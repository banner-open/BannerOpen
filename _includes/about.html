The 4 Majors

The original Banner Open was born in 2018, combinging the skill of Augusta National with the drinking of a leaving cert holiday in Santa Ponsa. The Banner Open continues a long and storied tradition of incredible golfing in Ireland.

After the resounding success of the second annual Banner Open which saw Tony Egan and Sean Russell take home the coveted Claret Jug(s), The Open has gone International. The Banner International takes place in the Algarve. 5 days of grueling pint consumption and training in the recovery facitlity at Vila Sol.

The Banner Invitational, the newest product from The Banner Group. Taking place on April 25th in Oughterard. Pints will be consumed away from the golf course as the athletes minds will he soley on the trophy, medals and prize money on offer at this prestigious event.

Talks are underway for a fourth major, watch this space.
