Ireland's Premier Golf Endurance Tournament

Team members are restricted from consuming their competition beverage until both team members first drives have left the first tee. Each team begins with a 20 drink-shot handicap, each shot equivalent to a 500 ml can at a minimum of 4% alcohol content. The handicap can be reduced by consuming beverages. Exceeding the equivalent of 20 cans will not reduce scores any futher. 1 shot will be deducted from a team that can consume 50% of their chosen alcohol before the 9th hole completes. Each team member is allowed one mulligan per the entire round Competitive drink consumption ends when a team completes the 18th hole. No further drinks will reduce drink-shot handicap
