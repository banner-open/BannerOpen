The Open Travels to Portugal

After the resounding success of the second annual Banner Open which saw Tony Egan and Sean Russell take home the coveted Claret Jug(s), The Open has gone international.

Taking place this August in Vilamoura Portugal, the golfers will undertake a 5 day endurance test in the midday Portugal sun.

Whittled down to 6 of the best and brightest Shannon has to offer, the competition will be split into a Ryder Cup style scramble and head to head competitions.

"I love pints and golf, I have a lot in common with these young athletes, at the top of their game for the last several years. A joy to behold." - Shane Lowry
